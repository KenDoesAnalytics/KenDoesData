Enhancing the Culinary Experience with Tableau

Within the Tableau Workbook, a meticulously organized menu is presented, accompanied by comprehensive dataset for each menu item, delving beyond taste to encompass ingredients, nutritional details, and preparation methods. This approach facilitates an understanding of the culture associated with the food, enhancing the dining experience. By utilizing Tableau's interactive visualizations, diners (otherwise known as my friends and family who have voluntarily agreed to risk their lives as my test tasters) can analytically engage with the menu, making well-informed choices that align with their dietary considerations.

For the Cooks: The Recipe and Shopping Lists presented at the end of the workbook is for utilization when planning, preparing and shopping. The primary-turned-secondary purpose of this project is to save time by filtering between recipes. Staying organized--especially when it comes to multiple recipes--benefits greatly by having:
(1) Recipe Prep List - Planning and Prep Work
(2) Shopping List - Shopping for ingredients via converted measures. Click here to view the raw Queried Excel Workbook.

Authorization Prep-Review in SQL

Data Cleaning & Transformation via Microsoft SQL Server. For Preparation of Authorization Review in a Clinical Setting.

Some Health Insurances require Approved Referrals/Authorizations for services to be received and paid through insurance. Patient's are responsible for Contracted Rates should Referral/Auth be Invalid or Absent. This subsequently leads to No Payment, Delayed Payment, and Loss of Business. This is one of the many hassles when working with health insurances. While it helps to get Authorizations and Referrals approved when possible, we all get bogged down with other tasks. I've had to recalibrate and set time aside for a whole 8-hour work day is required to get the majority of work completed.
